Hello, and welcome to GOA - Goal Oriented Academy

In a Digital ecosystem named GOA, teachers use algorithmic and strategic approaches
to educate and shape young minds . over time, the students learn to solve problems, write efficient code and
gain proficiency in leadership in GOA, the schedules of courses are tailored to each student, they get to choose
their timetable and choose select online or in-person courses, the latter held in Tbilisi, Georgia.

About GOA

As you might have guessed, this academy operates in the Georgian language so almost every student is
expected to know georgian. the founder of the Goal Oriented Academy
is Nika Keshelava, a Georgian digital creator, founding GOA in 2022 He has gained popularity ever since through
credible and trustworthy promotions on social media.

GOA Martial Arts

GOA also offers a newly launched Martial Arts Academy - GOA Martial Arts - which provides self-defense
training not only to GOA programming students but also to youngsters outside the GOA community.
professional trainers have been hired to deliver high-quality education to ambitious young fighters. This MMA Gym is located in the outskirts of Tbilisi, click here for more details.
